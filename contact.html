What to Include When You Reach Out

To help me give you a quick, accurate response, please include:

  • What you need: Bug fix, feature development, code review, retainer support?
  • Your tech stack: Laravel version, relevant dependencies, architecture
  • Timeline: When do you need this done?
  • Scope/budget: Roughly what are you expecting to spend?
  • Preference: How would you prefer to communicate?

But honestly, even just "I have a Laravel bug" is enough to get the conversation started.
